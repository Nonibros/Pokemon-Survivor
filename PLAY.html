A Simple Guide

This is a simple guide on how to play the Pokémon Firered hack, Pokémon Survivor, by Nonibros.

Download the current Patch

How to play!

Thank you for your interest in Pokémon Survivor!

To prevent issues with patching itself, I created this page to explain what tools are needed.

Disclaimer: This page is also here, linked from the home page, and not linked to the Pokecommunity site, so as to not anger any mods on the Pokecommunity whom understandably don't want to get in trouble because of suggesting a link to a website which has a ROM, due to legal trouble and what not.

With that said, lets begin.

How to Play

The first thing you'll need is the ROM.

Emuparadise is a fantastic site for any of your ROM needs, and it is where we will be obtaining our ROM.

The link to the website (and ROM) is located here.

Next you need Lunar IPS, which is located here and here.

You need to download the current patch and then patch it using Lunar IPS to the Firered ROM you downloaded.

That is literally it.

It could not be much simpler.

Afterwards

You're done! Get to playing!
